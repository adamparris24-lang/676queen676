Blood Moon Burial is a level 3 Dispersal of O.Y.O in 4 Phases.

It is currently in its first Phase.
This Dispersal requires
10 enactments to advance to its next Phase.
Phase 1:

I.

Gather the Talismans from the vessel. Ground your spirit upon the earth, feeling the coarse reality beneath the flesh. Before lighting the Special Candle, breathe in the stillness of the air until the silence itself becomes a hum.

II.

Arrange the Audio Medium centrally. Speak the name of the one who wanders. Play the recording thrice, each iteration softer than the last, allowing the decay of sound to stretch the boundaries of the room.

III.

Extinguish all artificial light. Only the candle's flame should guide your vision. Face the North, where the shadow lies heaviest, and chant the incantation of absolute release.

Phase 2:

I.

The Invokers must now anoint the threshold with the Blood Vial, marking a continuous line of intent. Strike the match. Let the Red Candles burn until the wax weeps onto the Bonedust scattered below.

II.

With Chalk, trace the outline of absence on the floor. Within the symbol, leave the Video Medium playing silently. Await the sign—a draft in a sealed room, or a flicker of the darkest flame.

III.

Once the sign manifests, clear the symbol without breaking the circle's boundary. Bury the chalk remnants in a place where nothing roots. Sleep deeply, and do not acknowledge the reflection in any mirror until dawn.
