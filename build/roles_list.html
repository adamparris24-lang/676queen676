Roles of O.Y.O

Running Count:
▶ Incinterator

The Incinterator is a master of cinders and ash, charged with the solemn duty of returning worldly elements to their base states through the purifying embrace of flame.

They act as catalysts of transformation. When artifacts and ethereal bindings have served their purpose, the Incinterator ensures their energies are released back into the void, preventing spiritual stagnation within the Archive.

▶ Watcher

Tethered to the boundary places, the Watcher maintains vigil over the thresholds of the Archive. Their sight pierces through veils, bearing witness to both the manifested and the ethereal.

They are the silent observers, recording shifts in resonance and documenting spectral anomalies. A Watcher never interferes directly but their vigilance acts as a shield against unraveling forces.

▶ Initiate

Standing at the precipice of esoteric knowledge, the Initiate begins the slow descent into the mysteries of the Ordo Yule Orphic. Their tasks are minor but absolutely crucial to the foundation of deeper rites.

Every Initiate must prove their attunement through service and alignment. They are the newly awakened, learning to navigate the frequencies and rhythms of the arcane currents.

▶ Archivist

The Archivist is the guardian of sequence and record. They catalog the influx of Gifts and ensure the Ledger remains an unbroken chain of causality.

Their precision is their power. To name a thing correctly is to bind it to the Archive forever.

▶ Scribe

The Scribe translates the ephemeral whispers of the Archive into the permanent ink of the Ledger. They capture the essence of Visitations and the intent of Dispersals.

Every stroke of their pen is a ritual in itself, a method of stabilizing the shifting realities of the O.Y.O.

▶ Herald

The Herald announces the shifting phases of the Archive to the outer world. They are the voice of the Ordo, calling participants to enact new rituals.

When the Great Dispersal calls, it is the Herald who sounds the resonance.
